PathoPhenoDB Search

A database of pathogens and their phenotypes for diagnostic support in infections.

Examples queries:

  • Example1(List all the pathogens with their disease-phenotypes)
  • Example2(List all disease with their phenotypes)
  • Example3(List all diseases which caused by pathogens)
  • Example4(List all diseases along with the drugs used to treat them)
  • Example5(List all the pathogens with their resistant proteins)
  • Example6(List all the pathogens with their resistant DNA accessions)
  • Example7(List all the pathogens along with the resistant drugs)
  • Example8(for a given specific pathogen, list all the diseases that it can cause)
SPARQL Endpoint: http://patho.phenomebrowser.net/sparql/.
